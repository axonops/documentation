AxonOps is available in two editions. The Free Edition gets you started on AxonOps Cloud, while Enterprise adds scale, retention, and optional managed services.

**Get started**

- [Free sign up](https://axonops.com/free)
- [Book an expert](https://axonops.com/book-an-expert/)

## At a glance

| Plan | Best for | Pricing | Limits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free Edition** | Trying AxonOps Cloud on smaller clusters | Free | Up to 6 nodes, 2 cloud users, 1-day metrics retention |
| **Enterprise Edition** | Production-scale operations and long retention | Contact sales | Unlimited nodes and users, extended retention, optional managed Cassandra |

## Retention policies

### Metrics retention policy

| Resolution | Free Edition | Enterprise Edition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 seconds | 1 day | 7 days |
| 1 minute | 1 day | 30 days |
| 12 minutes | 1 day | 60 days |
| 2 hours | 1 day | 180 days |
| 1 day | 1 day | 365 days |

### Event log retention policy

| Log type | Free Edition | Enterprise Edition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Operational logs | 1 day | 4 weeks |
| Audit logs | 1 day | 8 weeks |

## Need help choosing?

If you are operating production clusters, need long-term retention, or want optional managed Cassandra, Enterprise is the right fit. The Free Edition is ideal for evaluation and small cloud deployments.
